This is data from the Analog to Multiple Broadband Inventories collected on-line in 2019 with an interactive personality test. Users found the test while browsing the internet and took it with the motivation to receive personalized results. They agreed their anonymous data could be used for research and distrbuted.

For into about the AMBI see
(1) Yarkoni, T. (2010). The abbreviation of personality, or how to measure 200 personality scales with 200 items. Journal of Research in Personality, 44, 180-198.
(2) https://ipip.ori.org/AMBIScoringKeys.htm

Each item had 3 values recoreded for it:

A - the users answer:	1=Strongly disagree, 2=Disagree, 3=Slightly disagree, 4=Neutral, 5=Slightly agree, 6=Agree, 7=Strongly agree
I - the position in the survey that user got the item at
E - the time elapsed in milliseconds the user spent on that item



This dataset has been slightly cleaned. Records were removed where:
(1) total time spend was less than 181 seconds
(2) number of items where time spend by user was 500ms or less was no more than 1
(3) more than one test was submitted from the same network location
